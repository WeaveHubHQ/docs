# Vibe Badge

ESP32-powered wearable display that shows your current energy level. Controlled from the Vibe Badge iOS app or Shortcuts, with Bluetooth LE updates to the device.

## What it does

- Five emoji faces and a physical arrow to show your social energy, with light and dark display modes.
- Bluetooth control from the iOS app plus Shortcuts/Action button integration.
- Simple hardware: ESP32 with ST7789 TFT display.

## Get started

1. Flash the Vibe Badge firmware to an ESP32 board with an integrated ST7789 TFT display.
2. Install the Vibe Badge iOS app and pair with your device over Bluetooth.
3. Set your level with presets (Empty, 25%, 50%, 75%, Full), a slider, or a toggle action.
4. Optional: assign the Toggle action to your Action button for quick one-press updates.
