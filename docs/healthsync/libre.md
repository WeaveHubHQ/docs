# HealthSync: Libre (in development)

Retrieves blood glucose measurements from LibreLinkUp into Apple Health.

## LibreLinkUp Configuration

1. In the Libre app, open Settings > Connected Apps.
2. Add a new connection.
3. Enter an Email address to which you have access.
4. Download the LibreLinkUp app from the iOS app store.
5. Create an account and login with the email address you entered in step 3. You may need to accept an email invitation first. 

## Configure

1. Enable the **Libre** integration in HealthSync.
2. Sign in to your Libre account using the same email address above and grant blood glucose permission.
3. HealthSync writes readings into HealthKit; they sync to other enabled destinations automatically.
