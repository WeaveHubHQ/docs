# HealthSync: Libre (in development)

Pulls blood glucose from Libre into Apple Health so it can flow to Home Assistant and Google Sheets.

## Configure

1. Enable the **Libre** integration in HealthSync.
2. Sign in to your Libre account and grant blood glucose permission.
3. HealthSync writes readings into HealthKit; they sync to other enabled destinations automatically.
